About Us

Whiskey Soda Ltd. is a creative events company founded in 2025 with one simple idea: to blend the magic of cinema with the craft of cocktails. We design immersive screenings where every film is paired with a bespoke tasting menu of drinks inspired by key moments on screen.

Our Story

Born from a passion for storytelling and hospitality, Whiskey Soda began as a project to reimagine the movie-going experience. Instead of popcorn and soda, guests are treated to expertly crafted cocktails that echo the mood, setting, and characters of the film they’re watching.

Our Approach

  • Immersive Design: Every event is tailored to the film, from the drinks to the atmosphere.
  • Craft Cocktails: We use quality spirits, fresh ingredients, and inventive techniques to build flavors that tell a story.
  • Community: Our screenings are social experiences, bringing people together to share cinema, conversation, and creativity.

Our Vision

We believe cinema and cocktails are both about experience — and when paired together, they create unforgettable nights. Whether it’s a cult classic, an arthouse gem, or a holiday favorite, Whiskey Soda aims to make every screening an event worth remembering.
