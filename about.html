Cinema & Cocktails — Reimagined

Our Story

Whiskey Soda Ltd. was founded to bring a unique twist to the event experience. We combine cinema and cocktails to create immersive screenings where drinks are paired with key moments on screen.

Each tasting menu is carefully crafted to complement the story and evoke emotions from the film. From intimate screenings to larger events, we aim to transport our audience into the cinematic world, one sip at a time.

Our Mission

Our mission is to transform how people experience films. By blending storytelling with curated cocktails, we create memorable nights that engage all the senses.
